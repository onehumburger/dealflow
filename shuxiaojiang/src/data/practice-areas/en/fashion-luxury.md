- Provided general corporate services to a top luxury and fashion group headquartered in France.
- Project managed the rolling out of the new China business model of a world major diamond and jewellery company; drafted/reviewed licenses, management agreements and cooperation agreements; led the negotiation of major contracts with governmental jewellery appraisal institutions, major national jewellery retailers and major national financing groups.
- Advised a U.K. luxury brand on its licensing, manufacture and distribution in China.
- Advised a top menswear brand on the transition of its China business from licensing to franchising.
- Assisted a wide range of U.K. and European fashion brands with achieving master licence agreement, or setting up joint venture with its Chinese master licensee, for operation in the Greater China area.
- Managed trademark and copyright portfolio for various fashion clients in China; handled more than 135 trademark registrations and copyright registrations; project managed a number of CTMO proceedings and trademark litigations.
- Handled IP infringement matters for various fashion industry clients; project managed counterfeiting actions against more than 80 infringers.
